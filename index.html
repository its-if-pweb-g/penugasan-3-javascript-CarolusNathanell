Top 3 Movies / Series

Weathering with You - Tenki no Ko

Weathering with You (Tenki no Ko) was a very enjoyable theater experience, from the sound effects, the songs, and of course the visuals. Although some parts do feel a bit rushed, the overall drama buildup and subsequent climax was satisfying.

Spy x Family

Spy x Family was an interesting combination of genres, from action to slice of life. The absurdness of some of it's scenes makes it somewhat of a good comedy series. This absurdness also carries some serious tones of animal and human experimentations and the aftermath of a long war.

Edge of Tommorow

Edge of Tommorow has a nice premise, restarting the day after dying. Some of the restarts are morbidly kind of funny, though the majority of it contains some serious and grimy moments.
